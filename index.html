About Us

We are an organic farm in an urban environment. We aim to grow the healthiest, freshest food in harmony with nature and educate urban growers on our tried and tested practices through workshops, farm tours, and participation through volunteering.
We also sell our fresh, healthy organic produce to the public.

We guarantee we do not spray any harmful chemical insecticides or herbicides onto our plants. 
Our first line of defense is a balanced ecosystem with plant diversity, regenerative agriculture and bio-dynamics.   

We make our own natural fertilizers rather than using synthetic ones, and  
  feed a healthy living soil that's full of good microbes and fungi that look after the plants.
Then the nutrient-dense plants look after us! Following nature's way, the way it should be.
